Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about Kasi2Campus and the application process.

Chat with us on WhatsApp
What is Kasi2Campus?
Kasi2Campus is a student support service that helps learners apply to universities and colleges in South Africa. We handle the application process so you can focus on your studies.
Do you apply to universities on my behalf?
Yes. Depending on the package you choose, we submit applications for you and guide you throughout the process.
How many universities can I apply to?
It depends on the package you choose. For example, the Basic package allows 5 applications. Other packages may allow more and include extra services.
Can I apply if I don’t have my final matric results yet?
Yes, many universities allow Grade 12 learners to apply using latest available results (Grade 11 / Term results). Each university is different, and we guide you on what is accepted.
How do I know what course I qualify for?
Use our APS & Average Calculator on the website. We also help you choose courses based on your APS and subjects.
Do you help with NSFAS?
Yes, NSFAS support is available depending on your package. If your package doesn’t include it, you can add NSFAS support as an extra service.
Do you do follow-ups with universities?
Follow-ups depend on your package. If your package does not include follow-ups, you can add follow-ups as an extra service.
How do I pay?
You can pay via PayFast or using other payment options like EFT and PayShap. Visit the Contact page for full details. After payment, send proof of payment on WhatsApp.
What documents do I need?
Common requirements include:
  • ID Copy
  • Latest Results / School Report
  • Proof of Payment
  • Proof of Upgrading (if upgrading)
We will tell you exactly what is needed based on the institution.
How long does the application process take?
Application submission can be done quickly once we have your correct details and documents. University responses vary — some take days, others take weeks or months depending on the institution.
Do you guarantee acceptance?
No service can guarantee acceptance because universities make the final decision. However, we help ensure your applications are submitted correctly and on time.
How do I contact Kasi2Campus?
You can WhatsApp us on 066 468 7222 or email kasi2campus@gmail.com. Visit the Contact page for all details.
