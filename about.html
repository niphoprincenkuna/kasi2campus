About Kasi2Campus

Kasi2Campus is a proudly South African initiative founded and owned by Mr. N Nkuna, dedicated to bridging the gap between township learners and higher education opportunities. With a deep understanding of the challenges faced by students across the country, Kasi2Campus offers a streamlined, supportive platform for university applications, funding guidance, and academic planning. Our mission is to simplify the journey from high school to campus, making it accessible, affordable, and empowering for every learner. Whether you're applying to institutions, calculating your APS, or exploring career paths, Kasi2Campus is here to walk with you every step of the way.
